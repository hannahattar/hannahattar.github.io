How I think about quant problems

  • Separate prediction from deployment. Outcomes depend heavily on when to act, not only what a model outputs.
  • Assume regimes exist. If behavior shifts across states, pooled averages hide failure modes.
  • Prefer diagnostics over marginal accuracy. Calibration, reliability, and error anatomy matter.
  • Be conservative with probabilities. Low SNR compresses forecasts toward 0.5 and demands restraint.
  • Optimize for robustness. Stress-test assumptions and treat uncertainty as part of the system.
