Probability and Stochastic Processes

Probability and Stochastic Processes is a foundations text that develops a rigorous and conceptually unified treatment of probabilistic modeling and random dynamical systems. The book progresses from core principles of probability to stochastic processes and continuous-time models, with an emphasis on internal consistency, mathematical structure, and intuitive interpretation. By highlighting how uncertainty and information propagate over time, the text establishes a framework suitable for advanced quantitative modeling, including applications encountered in financial engineering and related disciplines.
